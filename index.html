COLOR TILES

Color tiles is a visual game which focuss on training your visual skill. Click on the correct tiles.

How to play

The game is divided into 3 difficulty mode;

GAME DIFFICULTY;

🔸 Junior: The easiest difficulty level ( age 5-10).
🔸 Senior: Moderately difficult (age 10-15).
🔸 Master: very challenging (age 15 and above).

🔸 The age doesn't really matter, the aim is to train yourself to be better than you presently are.
🔸 Changing the difficulty level will totally reset the game (scores).

NOTE

🔸 Opening the menu tab pauses the game and clicking resume continues the game
🔸 Change the background color by clicking "NIGHT MODE" button.
🔸 The progress tab (at the top) displays the current difficulty level, correct answers, wrong answers, and the remaining time respectively.
🔸 Don't worry too much cus you are only competing with yourself.
🔸 The correct answer will be shown if you eventually got it wrong.
